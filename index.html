How to get Ranked

The Jordanian Minecraft Tierlist ranking is determined by active testers using standardized test matches. To request a test:

  1. Join our official Discord server.
  2. Go to the #request-test channel.
  3. Select the gamemode you'd like to test in and ping an available Tester.
  4. Complete a Best-of-5 (BO5) or Best-of-9 (BO9) duel. The tester will decide your tier based on your mechanical skill, positioning, and overall performance.

Tier Distribution

Tiers are organized from Tier 1 (highest) to Tier 5 (lowest). Each tier has a High (HT) and Low (LT) variant:

HT1LT1

Master Class: Peak mechanics, national-level competitors.

HT2LT2

Elite Class: Extremely advanced fighters, server tournament regulars.

HT3LT3

Expert Class: Solid mechanics, reliable execution, well-above average.

HT4LT4

Intermediate Class: Decent capability, active player base, basic combos.

HT5LT5

Novice Class: Entry-level competitive PvP, developing movement and setups.
